Email Security Policy

Overview

Proper email security measures must be followed to prevent unauthorized access, phishing attacks, and data leaks.

Email Security Best Practices

  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for all email accounts.
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or attachments from unknown senders.
  • Use strong passwords and change them regularly.
  • Encrypt sensitive emails to protect data during transmission.
  • Monitor your inbox for any unusual activity.
